About ENDSCAPE

ENDSCAPE blends modern rock and metal with electronic textures, cinematic songwriting, and a digital edge. Built for loud rooms and dark stages, the project pushes high-energy hooks, heavy rhythm, and atmosphere that feels both futuristic and human.

From aggressive drops to melodic refrains, ENDSCAPE sits at the intersection of heavy music and electronic production. New releases, videos, and live dates are announced first through the mailing list.
